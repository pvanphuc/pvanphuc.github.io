### Matrix Multiplication

$$
A \in R^{m\times n}
$$

$$
B \in R^{n\times p}
$$

$$
C=AB\in R^{m\times p}
$$

Where:

$$
C_{ij}=\sum_{k=1}^{n}A_{ik}B_{kj}
$$
